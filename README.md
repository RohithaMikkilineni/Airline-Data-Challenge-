# Airline-Data-Challenge

## Problem Statement
The airline is looking to establish an initial presence in the US market by launching 5 strategically chosen routes, while ensuring they have the necessary aircraft to maintain a high level of on-time performance in line with their brand promise. The significant upfront investment in airplanes underscores the importance of careful route selection and operational efficiency for the success of this endeavor.

1.**Explore the data** to identify any data quality issues and challenges.

2.**Find Top 5 Beneficial Routes** based on model scores.

3.**Summarize the findings** and communicate any insights, trends and reccomendation for business development.
